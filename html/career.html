Leon

In 2009 I decided to join an Erasmus Program in Leon, city in the north and cold region of Castilla Leon where I study Tourism and learn how to speak Spanish. During this period I study with over 300 students from all continents of the world and I had an unique opportunity to experience to study and work in a worldwide community.
